# corporate_managers × anxiety × compression_distillation
# Slot type: COMPRESSION
# Count: 30
# Voice: present tense, one insight, no lecturing
# Spec: 40–120 words, 2–6 sentences, one insight, one reframe, TTS-safe

## COMPRESSION v01
---
compression_family: C1
---
Your body is ahead of your mind. The meeting isn't urgent but your shoulders are tight, your jaw is clenched, your breath is shallow. You're scanning for threats that your nervous system has already detected before your conscious mind catches up. The insight is that anxiety isn't a thinking problem—it's a sensing problem. Your body has learned to prepare for the worst because the worst has happened before. The reframe: you're not broken. You're hypervigilant.

## COMPRESSION v02

In version three, something you said last quarter comes up and everyone realizes you didn't have the full answer. In version five, you're asked to defend a decision and you freeze. In version seven, you say nothing and that silence is interpreted as doubt. The insight is that anxiety is your mind trying to control the future by rehearsing failure. The reframe: you can't predict what will happen.
