

## EXERCISE v01: The 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding at Your Desk
---

---
Sit at your desk. Place both feet flat on ground. Name five things you see. Say them aloud slowly. Name four things you can touch. Touch each one deliberately. Name three things you hear. Listen carefully to each. Name two things you smell. Breathe in fully twice. Name one thing you taste. Stay with this one. Open your eyes if closed. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for four counts. Breathe out for four counts. Return to your work.
---

## EXERCISE v02: The Grounding Press in Conference Room
---

---
Stand up from the conference room table. Push both palms against the table firmly. Press down hard for five counts. Release completely. Press again down hard. Release completely. Press down a third time. Your feet are on the ground. Feel your feet very clearly. Wiggle all ten toes deliberately. Press them into the floor. Breathe in for four counts. Breathe out for six counts. Sit back down slowly. You are still here.
---

## EXERCISE v03: Car Commute Breathing Anchor
---

---
Pull over to a safe location. Keep your hands on the wheel. Breathe in slowly for four counts. Hold the breath for four counts. Breathe out slowly for six counts. Pause for two counts. Repeat this five times. Focus on the breath. Notice the steering wheel under your hands. Notice the seat beneath you. When ready, continue driving. The grounding continues in motion.

Walk into the room.

## EXERCISE v24: The Counting Technique for Spiraling Thoughts
---

---
Your thoughts are spiraling. Count backwards from twenty. Count slowly. With each number, breathe out. When you reach zero, breathe in deeply. Count backwards from twenty again. You're interrupting the spiral pattern. You're introducing a new pattern. The spiral loses power. The counting gains power. Do this twice. The spiral breaks. Your mind settles. Breathe normally now.
