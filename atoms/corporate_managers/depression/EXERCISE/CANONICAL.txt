

## EXERCISE v01: The Minimum Viable Day
---

---
Today, you only need three things. Stand up. Eat one meal. Move your body for five minutes. That's your whole day. Anything beyond that is bonus. You're not expecting productivity. You're expecting survival. Three things. That's enough. You're doing great.
---

## EXERCISE v02: The Physical Activation Wake-Up
---

---
Lie in bed. Stretch your arms above your head. Hold for ten seconds. Release. Stretch your legs out. Hold for ten seconds. Release. Wiggle every body part. Make yourself a little sore. Movement wakes your nervous system. Your body is waking. Your mind will follow.
---

## EXERCISE v03: The Shower Reset
---

---
Take a shower. Specifically, let the water hit your face. Open your mouth. Taste the water. Feel the temperature. Let the water run down your neck. Be very present in the shower. Your body is present. Your mind is in the shower. Not in the flatness. Five minutes minimum. You're real. The water is real.
---
