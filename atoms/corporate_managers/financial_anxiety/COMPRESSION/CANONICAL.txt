# Slot type: COMPRESSION
# Count: 30

## COMPRESSION v01
compression_family: C1
Your salary hit a new peak this year but something shifted. You're comparing it to peers in similar titles at other firms. The gap feels like evidence of underperformance. The bonus you once celebrated is now the baseline you expect. Without it, there's a shortfall you can't quite articulate to your partner. The number you have never matches the number you need to feel secure.

## COMPRESSION v02
compression_family: C2

Your compensation is tied to metrics that depend on decisions you don't fully control. This creates a constant low-frequency alert in your nervous system. Safety has become conditional.
