What I notice is a particular erosion that happens at the edge of the work day. A student stays after class to talk about something difficult. A parent emails after hours about a concern. A colleague needs something done before tomorrow. The moment arrives where I could draw a line between the work and the rest of my life, and the line does not get drawn. The mechanism I recognize is what I think of as default-to-availability.
