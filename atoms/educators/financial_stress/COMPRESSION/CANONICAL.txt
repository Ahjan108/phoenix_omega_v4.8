## COMPRESSION v01
---
compression_family: C1
---
The mechanism is that your income has become insufficient for basic living. So you work a second job. You grade papers at night. You teach summer school. You do test prep on weekends. The teaching itself is not enough to sustain you financially. So you have become multiple people doing multiple jobs and none of them are paying well enough. This is the trap. You are working twice as hard and still falling behind.

---

## COMPRESSION v02
---
compression_family: C2
---
The cost of financial stress is that you cannot afford to take care of yourself. You skip the doctor. You buy generic groceries. You cannot afford good shoes so your back hurts. You cannot afford therapy so you suffer alone. The financial stress has become a health issue. The health issue compounds the financial stress. You are spiraling downward and the system that is supposed to value you is contributing to the spiral.

## COMPRESSION v03
---
compression_family: C3
---
Your body carries the financial stress as a baseline anxiety. Your nervous system is in low-level alert mode constantly. You do not sleep well because part of you is calculating bills. You do not relax because relaxation feels like irresponsibility. Your stomach is always slightly tight. Your shoulders are always slightly high. This is what chronic financial stress does to the animal body. It puts you in permanent mobilization.
