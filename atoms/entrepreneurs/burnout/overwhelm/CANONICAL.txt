## STORY v01
---
path: the inbox that stopped closing
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
You open your email at 6am and there are 47 new messages. By lunch there are 12 more. By midnight you haven't actually responded to any of them, just flagged some, archived others, and left the rest sitting there like evidence of a decision you can't make. You notice this. The noticing is quiet. You think maybe you should delegate more. Then you remember that delegating takes more explanation than just doing it yourself. So you keep going. The loop is starting. Not running yet. Just starting. You haven't named it a problem. You're calling it normal.
