You built this so your team would have a place. You hired people you believed in, paid them decently when you could barely pay yourself, answered 2 AM Slack messages about their career doubts. The irony is, you built the system so well that your team started to believe you actually had capacity for all of this. They believed you could hold it forever. And you kept performing that you could, long past the moment when it became true.
