

## EXERCISE v30
The Integration of Uncertainty. Step 1: Entrepreneurship requires decision-making with incomplete information. Step 2: You can either: a) overthink trying to complete the information, or b) make peace with the incompleteness and decide anyway. Step 3: The second option is where real entrepreneurship is. Step 4: Practice it. Small decisions first. Then bigger ones. Step 5: You'll be surprised how often it works out. Not perfectly. But fine.
