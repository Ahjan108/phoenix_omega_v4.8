# entrepreneurs × overthinking × the loop that won't shut off
# Slot type: INTEGRATION
# Count: 7

---
## INTEGRATION v01
mode: STILL-HERE
You're the founder. The weight is real. The decisions matter. The thinking is happening. And you're also still here, still functioning, still moving forward even with the thinking in the background. The decision got made. You're executing on it. The thinking is running in the other thread. That's fine. You don't have to shut it off. You just have to not let it paralyze you.

---
## INTEGRATION v02
mode: COST-VISIBLE
The cost of the overthinking is visible: sleep, peace, presence with your team, presence with people you care about. The benefit is minimal. The fear that the overthinking is protecting you is probably unfounded—you've built something real, so you're not actually incompetent. The protection you think the overthinking provides is mostly illusion. See that cost-benefit clearly.

---
## INTEGRATION v03
mode: BODY-LANDED
Your body is carrying the weight of the business. The tension is real. Your shoulders are tight. Your sleep is poor. Your nervous system is activated. The body is telling you something: this load is heavy. Listen to the body. It's not lying about the cost, even if your mind is in denial about it.

---
## INTEGRATION v04
mode: QUESTION-OPEN
Instead of "Did I make the right decision?" (which spirals), ask: "Is the business moving in the right direction?" or "Are we learning?" These are answerable. They point forward instead of backward. The answers are usually yes, which means the overthinking about the past decision is not serving anything.

---
## INTEGRATION v05
mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
If a founder you respected was doing what you're doing—second-guessing a solid decision, unable to move past it—what would you tell them? You'd tell them: commit. Execute. The decision was good. The overthinking is the enemy now, not the decision. You'd tell them to trust themselves. Tell yourself the same thing.

---
## INTEGRATION v06
mode: STILL-HERE
The isolation is real but not necessary. You're choosing to carry this alone. You could share it. You could let someone else in. The weight would not disappear, but it would be distributed. You don't have to overthink in isolation. Connection is available.

---
## INTEGRATION v07
mode: COST-VISIBLE
Every hour you spend overthinking a past decision is an hour not spent building the future. You're bleeding time toward something immutable. The opportunity cost is enormous. See it. Let it drive your decision to stop.
