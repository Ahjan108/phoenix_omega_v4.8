## STORY v01
---
path: founder detachment observation
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
I'm watching my company like it's someone else's company. Like I'm an observer of an experiment in entrepreneurship. The experiment is interesting. The experiment is proceeding. The experimenter—me—is executing the plan. And I'm watching the execution with curious distance.

This used to be the thing that mattered most. This company. This vision. This was the whole identity. But now I'm watching it happen from a place of separation. The watcher is interested. The watcher is not invested. The watcher is documenting the journey. But the journey is happening to someone else.

That someone else happens to be me, but the separation feels real. So the work gets done by the person-who-is-me, and the watching is done by the watcher-who-is-me, and they're no longer the same thing.
