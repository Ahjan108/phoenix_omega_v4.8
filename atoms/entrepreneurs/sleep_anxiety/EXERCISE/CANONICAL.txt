

## EXERCISE v30
Step 1: You're going to be tired tomorrow. Acknowledge this now. You're awake. You'll be tired. This is the trade you're making.

Step 2: Ask yourself if this trade is necessary. Is there something that actually requires your sleeplessness right now. Usually the answer is no.

Step 3: If it's no, your permission to sleep becomes permission to rest, becomes permission to stop. Your nervous system waits for you to stop. Stop.
