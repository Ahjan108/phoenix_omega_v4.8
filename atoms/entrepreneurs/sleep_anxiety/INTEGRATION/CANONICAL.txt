# entrepreneurs × sleep_anxiety × the mind that won't clock out
# Slot type: INTEGRATION
# Count: 7
---
## INTEGRATION v01
mode: STILL-HERE
You're awake at 2 AM and that's not going to change right now. Your nervous system is going to stay alert. That's not failure. That's just where you are. Stop fighting the 2 AM and let your body be awake while your mind settles. The business will still be there. You will still be here. Both are true. Both can exist without you solving anything. You're just here. In the dark. Awake. That's enough.

---
## INTEGRATION v02
mode: COST-VISIBLE
Every time you lose sleep over a metric you can't control tonight, your decision-making tomorrow costs you two more hours of sleep. The price is visible now. The runway worry cost you four hours. The competitor news cost you two more. You can afford some of this. Not all of it. The question isn't whether to sleep. It's whether tonight's worry is worth tomorrow's deficit. Usually it isn't.

---
## INTEGRATION v03
mode: BODY-LANDED
Your nervous system learned to run on adrenaline. Your body learned to ignore shutdown signals. This isn't your weakness. This is the cost of what you've built. Now your body needs to learn a new pattern. Not rest instead of work. Rest as part of work. Your shoulders need permission to drop. Your breath needs permission to deepen. Your eyes need permission to close. Give them that permission. The business survives.

---
## INTEGRATION v04
mode: QUESTION-OPEN
What if the business isn't in danger. What if the danger is only in the story your nervous system tells at 3 AM. What if the runway is actually fine and your anxiety is just old. What if the competitor doesn't matter yet. What if the investor was satisfied and you misread the silence. What if you're actually okay. Don't answer these questions. Just let them sit. Let your nervous system wonder if the fear is accurate.

---
## INTEGRATION v05
mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
Your co-founder is sleeping right now. They're carrying the same business weight. Their runway is the same. Their competitor threat is identical. But they're asleep. This means sleep is a choice, not a requirement of remaining vigilant. You can choose it. Your absence from the worry won't change the outcome. The outcome will still be whatever it's going to be. You might as well sleep through some of it.

---
## INTEGRATION v06
mode: STILL-HERE
Three AM thinks everything is urgent. Morning knows that most things can wait. You don't need to solve this now. You need to let your nervous system know that waiting is safe. That not solving it in the dark is a strategy, not a failure. You're here. The morning will arrive. Your nervous system can rest before then. It doesn't need to earn permission. It can just stop and wait.

---
## INTEGRATION v07
mode: COST-VISIBLE
Calculate what a rested version of you can accomplish versus what a sleep-deprived version can do. The rested version closes deals. The sleep-deprived version second-guesses the deals they should close. The rested version sees opportunity. The depleted version sees only risk. Sleep isn't a luxury. It's a business asset. Every hour you steal from sleep, you're stealing from your own performance. The math is that simple.
