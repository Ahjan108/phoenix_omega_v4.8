

## EXERCISE v30
Step 1: Identify what you need to believe to ask for help. For you it might be that asking doesn't mean quitting. That help doesn't mean weakness. Name that belief.

Step 2: Find one person you trust enough to test that belief with. One conversation where you say the real thing instead of the station thing.

Step 3: You don't have to fix anything in that conversation. You just have to prove to yourself that saying the truth out loud is possible and you survive it.
