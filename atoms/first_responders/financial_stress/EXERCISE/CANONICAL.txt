

## EXERCISE v30
Step 1: Set a date six months from now. Write down what you want to be true about the financial situation by then.

Step 2: Write down what you're willing to change to make that true. What you'll say yes to. What you'll say no to.

Step 3: You don't have to do this. But having a target instead of just reacting to bills changes how you move. That's the work.
