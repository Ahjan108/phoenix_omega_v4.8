# first_responders × grief × the losses that don't get a funeral
# Slot type: EXERCISE
# Count: 30

---
## EXERCISE v01
Step 1: Name one person who used to work with you who is no longer at the station. They don't have to be dead. They could have transferred or quit or moved on.

Step 2: What do you miss about that person. Not everything. Just one specific thing.

Step 3: You're allowed to miss them even though they're fine. That's grief and it's real. Name it. That person mattered.

## EXERCISE v02
Step 1: Identify the call that replays. The one you come back to. The one that doesn't resolve.

Step 2: Write down what you would have done if you could do it again. Be specific. Not the whole thing. Just the moment.

Step 3: That replaying is how your mind processes unfinished business. It's not a sign you failed. It's a sign that call matters. You're grieving what happened. That's normal.
