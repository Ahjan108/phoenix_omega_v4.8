# Slot type: EXERCISE

## EXERCISE v01
Step 1: Write down what you're good at. Be specific. Not the job skills. The actual skills. Physical. Mental. Relational. Write them.

Step 2: Now write down which of those skills are useful outside the job. Which ones matter in the regular world.

Step 3: You're looking at your value outside the uniform. It's probably bigger than you think. That's useful information.

---
## EXERCISE v02
Step 1: Identify what would be lost if you left the job tomorrow. The paycheck. The structure. The identity. Write it.

Step 2: Now identify what wouldn't be lost. The skills. The relationships outside the job. The person you are at home. Write that.

Step 3: You're not the job. You're a person with a job. The difference is important. Hold it.
