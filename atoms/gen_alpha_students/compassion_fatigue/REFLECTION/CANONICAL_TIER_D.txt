## REFLECTION v01

voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: protective
tier_variant: D

I have noticed that compassion fatigue often arrives quietly. You have been the person your friends text when things fall apart. You have been listening. You have been present. You have been the steady one. And then one day you realize that there is no one listening to you. That you are not present to yourself. That you are not steady anymore. You are running on empty.

Your parent is unhappy, and understanding them will not make them happy. Your classmate is lonely, and your friendship will not solve the loneliness. The suffering continues regardless of how much you care. The magic was never available in the first place.
