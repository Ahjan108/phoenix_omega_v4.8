Maya watches her friend cry and notices she's watching her friend cry instead of feeling it with her. Her hand is on her friend's shoulder. Her eyes are seeing tears. Her body is positioned for comfort. But inside, Maya is narrating: she's crying, I'm comforting, this is sad. The narration creates a gap between Maya and the sadness.
