## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: automatic
tier_variant: C

Your nervous system fires at full intensity before you speak. The fear is real. The threat perception is wrong. The mechanism does not distinguish. It waits for certainty that might never arrive. But the action can still happen. Courage can still exist. Waiting for the fear to disappear means waiting forever. The fear might not go away. So you move forward while afraid. Fear and courage become companions.
---

## REFLECTION v02
---
family: F2
voice_mode: companion
mechanism_emphasis: protective
tier_variant: C

Courage is not the absence of fear. The people who are actually courageous are often terrified. But they do the thing anyway. Fear and action coexist. The mechanism has learned to interpret fear as a stop sign. If you are afraid, do not try. But fear is information, not direction. Fear is telling you something matters to you. Fear is not telling you to stop. You can raise your hand with a shaking voice. You can speak up with a racing heart. You can try despite the terror that something might go wrong.
