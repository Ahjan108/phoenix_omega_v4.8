## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: automatic
tier_variant: C

You are aware that the future has a price. That your family has limits. That resources are not unlimited. The mechanism calculates: is this worth the money. The stakes feel high. The possibilities feel limited. The mechanism reads financial constraint as a permanent condition of your life. You have not even started working and you already feel the weight of needing to earn enough to survive.

## REFLECTION v02
---
family: F2
voice_mode: companion
mechanism_emphasis: protective
tier_variant: C

Financial anxiety arrives before you have any money of your own. You overhear conversations about college costs. You notice your parents stressed about bills. You become aware that money runs out. The mechanism internalizes the scarcity before you have earned your first dollar. The inherited anxiety becomes your anxiety. You start to carry it, feel responsible for it, believe it is your job to manage the financial stress that was never your job to manage. The burden arrives when you are still young, when you are still supposed to have possibilities.
