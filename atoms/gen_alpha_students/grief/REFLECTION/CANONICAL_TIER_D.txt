I have noticed that student grief is often invisible because you are supposed to keep going. Your grandparent dies and you still have to go to school. Your friend moves away and you still have to show up for class. Your future changes and you still have to do your homework. The world does not pause while you grieve. There is a mechanism called stoic continuation, and what it does is allow you to function while you are broken.
