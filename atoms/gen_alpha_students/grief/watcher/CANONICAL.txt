Maya's grandmother dies. The funeral is Saturday. Maya stands between her parents and watches the service. The watching is literal—she sees the casket, the flowers, the people crying. Maya identifies the watcher's trigger specificity. The watcher doesn't activate on all emotions. Maya feels anger—hot, immediate, physical. Maya feels excitement—buzzing, expanding, energetic. The watcher activates specifically on grief and sadness. The watcher intercepts the emotions that require vulnerability—the emotions that open the body instead of armoring it. Anger armors.
