## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: invisible
tier_variant: D

I have noticed that self-worth in students is often directly tied to academic performance. A good grade means you are good. A bad grade means you are bad. The grade is not feedback about one moment of performance. The grade is feedback about who you are as a person. There is a mechanism called identity fusion, and what it does is fuse the grade with the self. The mechanism is not separating the performance from the person.
