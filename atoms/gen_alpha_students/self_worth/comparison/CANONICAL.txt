Jaden realizes his worth has no internal anchor. When he's alone in his room drawing, his worth is stable—he likes his work, he sees improvement, he feels competent. The moment he enters a social context with other artists, the worth becomes dependent. The dependence is the problem—worth that requires favorable comparison to exist isn't worth. It's ranking.
