There was a version of this I carried for years. Not a specific plan exactly, but a shape. The idea that by this point I'd feel like I was building something. Not even anything dramatic. Just a request that felt normal to everyone else. But something in me recognized it as the moment the job stopped being something I was choosing and became something I was enduring. And I can't quite grieve it because I'm still supposed to be grateful that the job exists.
