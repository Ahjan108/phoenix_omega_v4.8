voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: protective
tier_variant: D

The specific kind of exhaustion that arrives in the first years of professional life is not usually from the work itself. It is from the emotional labor that lives inside the work. You are not just doing your job. You are managing how you appear while doing your job. You are reading the room. You are calibrating your tone. You are managing other people's feelings about your ideas, your presence, your questions.
