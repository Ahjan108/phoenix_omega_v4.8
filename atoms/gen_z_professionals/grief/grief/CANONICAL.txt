Jordan discovers grief comes in waves, not stages. Monday he's fine—productive, functional, clear. Tuesday the heaviness returns at lunch, unprompted, like weather. Wednesday he laughs at a colleague's joke and then feels guilty for laughing. Thursday he forgets to grieve and the forgetting feels like betrayal. The waves don't progress.
