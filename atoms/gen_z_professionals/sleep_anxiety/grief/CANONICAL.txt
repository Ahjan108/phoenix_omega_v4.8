# gen_z_professionals × sleep_anxiety × grief

GRIEF engine for sleep_anxiety: Grief for the years they used to fall asleep easily. Grief for the version of themselves that didn't know what "sleep hygiene" was because they didn't need to. Grief for the easy nights that stopped. Grief that the body that used to recover now requires management. For gen_z professionals, this grief comes early — at 24 or 25 they're already mourning the ease of college sleep.
