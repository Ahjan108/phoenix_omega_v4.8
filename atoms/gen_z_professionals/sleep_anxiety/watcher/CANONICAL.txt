# gen_z_professionals × sleep_anxiety × watcher

WATCHER engine for sleep_anxiety: The watcher observes the lying-awake self. "I'm awake. I'm still awake. It's 2am and I'm still awake." The watcher becomes the mechanism — the moment they notice they're awake, they're fully awake. The watcher watches them try every technique (counting, breathing, no screens) and notes each failure. The watcher is the reason sleep gets harder: the observation itself is the interference.
