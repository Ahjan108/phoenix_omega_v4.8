# gen_z_professionals × social_anxiety × spiral

SPIRAL engine for social_anxiety: The post-interaction spiral. They said something in the meeting — was it stupid? Did people notice? What did that look mean? The spiral replicates the interaction dozens of times, each replay finding new evidence of failure. For gen_z professionals, the spiral often happens via text review: re-reading Slack messages they sent, parsing emoji responses, analyzing reply speed. Digital traces make the spiral replicable.
