## Mechanism_Proof v01
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism of false alarm is the mechanism of threat misidentification. Alex's nervous system was conditioned at some point to read certain signals as dangerous. The signal might be: urgent email, elevated stakes, performance pressure, attention from authority. The nervous system learned to respond to these signals with full threat activation. The mechanism was adaptive at one point — it kept Alex alert in genuinely dangerous situations. But now the mechanism fires in response to normal professional situations. The destabilization emerges because the response is disproportionate to the actual threat. Alex is exhausted by the constant false alarms. The nervous system cannot rest because it's constantly detecting emergencies that don't exist.
