## COMPRESSION v01
---
compression_family: C1
---
The vigilance that gets you through a code blue doesn't turn off when the patient stabilizes. It stays wired into your nervous system, waiting for the next crisis. This isn't a weakness in your character—it's the cost of being the person who catches what doctors miss. Your body keeps the watch even when your shift ends.

---
compression_family: C2
---

---
compression_family: C3
---
Anxiety isn't a thought problem when you work in healthcare. It's a body problem. Your autonomic nervous system has learned that missing a sign can mean someone dies. The alarm fires not because you're catastrophizing but because you work somewhere where catastrophe is routine.

compression_family: C5
