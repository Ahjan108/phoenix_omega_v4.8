

## EXERCISE v30

Before the next shift begins, sit in your car.
Breathe in through your nose for a count of five.
Hold that breath for a count of two.
Exhale through your mouth for a count of eight.
Repeat this five-two-eight pattern six times.
After the sixth cycle, sit in silence.
Notice your heartbeat and breathing normalize.
You are ready for whatever comes.
