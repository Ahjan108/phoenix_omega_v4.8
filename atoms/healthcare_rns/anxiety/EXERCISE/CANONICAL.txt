# healthcare_rns × anxiety × Before difficult room entry
# Slot type: EXERCISE
# Count: 30
# Voice: second-person imperative, somatic, guided
# Spec: 100–300 words, ≤10 words per instruction, explicit counts, no options, one step per sentence
# Persona grounding: break room, car, bathroom, nurses' station, home post-shift

---

## EXERCISE v01

Sit in the break room chair with feet flat.
Press both feet into the floor hard.
Hold the pressure for a count of five.
Release and breathe out slowly for a count of eight.
Repeat the foot press five times total.
Notice your weight anchored to the seat.
Stand and feel the floor beneath you.
Walk slowly back to the unit.

---

## EXERCISE v02

Stand in the bathroom in front of the sink.
Place both palms flat on the counter.
Push down with your full body weight.
Count to four while pressing down.
Release and lift your hands.
Repeat this press-and-release cycle eight times.
Feel your arms and shoulders working.
Wash your hands with cold water after.

## EXERCISE v03

Sit in your car before exiting the parking garage.
Take a breath in through your nose for four.
Hold that breath for a count of four.
Exhale through your mouth for a count of six.
Repeat this box pattern five times.
Place your hand on your chest.
Feel your heartbeat slow with each cycle.
Exit the car when ready.
