## COMPRESSION v01

Depression in nurses isn't sadness. It's the numbness that comes after caring too much for too long without refilling. The flatness, the sense that nothing matters anymore, the exhaustion that sleep doesn't fix—these are the mechanics of depression in a high-stakes profession.
