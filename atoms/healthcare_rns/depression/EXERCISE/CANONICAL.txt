# healthcare_rns × depression × Activating presence when numb
# Slot type: EXERCISE
# Count: 30
# Voice: second-person imperative, somatic, guided
# Spec: 100–300 words, ≤10 words per instruction, explicit counts, no options, one step per sentence
# Persona grounding: break room, car, bathroom, nurses' station, home post-shift

---

## EXERCISE v01

At shift start when you feel nothing.
Sit in the break room on a hard chair.
Feel the cold chair beneath you.
Press your sitting bones down hard.
Say silently: I am here in this body.
Hold the press for a count of eight.
Release and press again.
Repeat this press-and-release cycle six times.

---

## EXERCISE v02

In the bathroom, stand in front of the sink.
Splash cold water on your face and neck.
Let the cold shock your system.
Feel the water dripping down your neck.
Say silently: I feel this water.
Run your wrists under cold water.
Count to ten while cold touches you.
Pat yourself dry slowly.

Your body is here.
