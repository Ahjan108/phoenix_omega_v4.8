# healthcare_rns × financial_stress × Grounding outside financial spiral
# Slot type: EXERCISE
# Count: 30
# Voice: second-person imperative, somatic, guided
# Spec: 100–300 words, ≤10 words per instruction, explicit counts, no options, one step per sentence
# Persona grounding: break room, car, bathroom, nurses' station, home post-shift

---

## EXERCISE v01

Before making an overtime decision, sit down.
Place both feet flat on the floor.
Press your feet down hard.
Say silently: My body does not have to work.
Count to six while pressing.
Release and press again.
Repeat this cycle six times.
This decision is about survival, not worth.

---

## EXERCISE v02

In the break room when financial worry hits.
Sit on a hard chair.
Feel the chair supporting your weight.
Say silently: This chair holds me without my labor.
Press your sitting bones down for six.
Release completely.
Repeat this support-and-release cycle five times.
You are held regardless of money.

## EXERCISE v03

In the bathroom before checking your account.
Splash cold water on your face.
Let the cold shock interrupt the spiral.
Say silently: This moment is separate from my bills.
Feel the cold for a count of six.
Pat yourself dry.
The water does not care about money.
You are here, alive, separate.
