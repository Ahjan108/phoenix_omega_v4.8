# healthcare_rns × grief × Permitted pause for patient loss
# Slot type: EXERCISE
# Count: 30
# Voice: second-person imperative, somatic, guided
# Spec: 100–300 words, ≤10 words per instruction, explicit counts, no options, one step per sentence
# Persona grounding: break room, car, bathroom, nurses' station, home post-shift

---

## EXERCISE v01

After patient death, sit in break room alone.
Place both feet flat on the floor.
Feel the weight of your entire body.
Say silently: I witnessed a life end today.
Press down into the seat for a count of ten.
Feel the reality of the chair.
Hold this position for two minutes.
You have earned this pause.

---

## EXERCISE v02

In the bathroom after loss, splash cold water.
Let the water run over your face.
Say silently: I am still alive.
Feel the cold shock fully.
Pat your face dry slowly.
Look at yourself in the mirror.
Meet your own eyes for ten seconds.
You are permitted to have witnessed this.

---

## EXERCISE v03

In your car after a patient dies.
Sit in the driver's seat.
Place both hands on the steering wheel.
Say silently: I held someone's hand until the end.
Grip the wheel for a count of six.
Release your grip.
Repeat this squeeze-and-release cycle five times.
Your hands did compassionate work.

---

## EXERCISE v04

In the break room, lie on the floor.
Feel your entire back on the ground.
Say silently: The ground holds all weight.
Press your spine down for a count of ten.
Feel the earth supporting you.
Lie here for three minutes in silence.
Tears may come.
This is permitted.

## EXERCISE v05

Say silently: I am still standing.

## EXERCISE v06

In the bathroom, sit on the toilet.
Lean forward and place your head on your thighs.
Say silently: I grieve this loss.
Hold this folded position for a count of ten.
Breathe slowly into your lap.
If tears come, let them fall.
Sit here for as long as needed.
Grief is the price of care.

## EXERCISE v07

In your car in the parking lot.
Sit in the driver's seat with eyes closed.
Say silently: This person mattered.
Breathe in through your nose for a count of five.
Hold for two.
Breathe out for a count of six.
Repeat this five-two-six breathing for three minutes.
You can carry this loss.
