The observer part watching the thinker part spiral endlessly. And the observer part is just narrating. It's not helping. It's not interrupting. It's just documenting my own descent into overthinking. And that detachment is its own form of suffering.

I'm overthinking everything including my own thoughts. I'm in a meta-loop where I'm thinking about how I'm thinking. And I can't get back to the simple act of just thinking. Just being. Just experiencing. I'm always one step removed.
