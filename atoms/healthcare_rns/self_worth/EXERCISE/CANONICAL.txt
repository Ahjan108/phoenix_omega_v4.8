# healthcare_rns × self_worth × Grounding outside hierarchical signal
# Slot type: EXERCISE
# Count: 30
# Voice: second-person imperative, somatic, guided
# Spec: 100–300 words, ≤10 words per instruction, explicit counts, no options, one step per sentence
# Persona grounding: break room, car, bathroom, nurses' station, home post-shift

---

## EXERCISE v01

After physician dismissal, sit in break room.
Place both feet flat on the floor.
Press your feet down with your full weight.
Say silently: The floor values me equally.
Hold the foot-press for a count of ten.
Release and press again.
Repeat five times.
The earth does not rank your worth.
