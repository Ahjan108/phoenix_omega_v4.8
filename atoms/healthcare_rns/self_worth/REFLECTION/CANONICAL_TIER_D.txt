## REFLECTION v01

voice_mode: teacher

I want to name something about the way self-worth in nursing becomes completely entangled with the capacity to help others. Before nursing, you had a self that was separate from helping. You had worth because you were a person. But nursing teaches you that your worth is specifically located in the helping. Your worth is how much you care. Your worth is how much you sacrifice. Your worth is how much you are needed.
