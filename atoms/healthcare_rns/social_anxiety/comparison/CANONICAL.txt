There's a charge nurse meeting today and I'm already anxious. I'm going to be in a room with fifteen other nurses. I'm going to have to listen. Respond. Maybe defend something I did. Or explain something that didn't go well. And I'm comparing myself to the other nurses in the room. Some of them are confident. They speak up. They have opinions. They don't seem afraid. And I'm sitting there feeling smaller. Feeling less articulate. Feeling like whatever I say won't be as good as what they're saying. So I don't speak up. I listen. I take notes. I make myself smaller. And the whole time I'm comparing my internal experience to their external confidence. And I'm losing the comparison. By the end of the meeting I feel like I've been silent when I should have spoken. And the silence has confirmed what I suspected. That I'm less competent than the people around me.
