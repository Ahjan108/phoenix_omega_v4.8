# millennial_women × anxiety × compression distillation
# Slot type: COMPRESSION
# Count: 30
# Voice: present tense, one insight, no lecturing
# Spec: 40–120 words, 2–6 sentences, one insight, one reframe, TTS-safe

---
## COMPRESSION v01
---
compression_family: C1
---
Anxiety lives in the future. It's your nervous system saying something bad might happen tomorrow, next week, next year. The body responds as if the threat is real right now. Your heart races, your shoulders tense. But the threat hasn't arrived. It exists only in the story your mind is telling. The insight: you're fighting an imaginary enemy. The reframe: this moment, right now, is fine. The future hasn't happened yet.

---
## COMPRESSION v02
---
compression_family: C2
---
The cost of anxiety is that it taxes your nervous system constantly. Vigilance is exhausting. Checking things over and over is exhausting. Living in alert mode is exhausting. You're burning energy on a threat that rarely materializes. Meanwhile, you're too tired to do the things that actually matter. The insight: anxiety is a protection that costs more than the danger it prevents. The reframe: you can survive without constant vigilance.
