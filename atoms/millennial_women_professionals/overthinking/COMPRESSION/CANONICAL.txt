## COMPRESSION v01
compression_family: C1
The overthinking is loyalty to your own standards dressed up as productivity. You think more about whether you did it right than you think about actually doing the next thing. The loop is the price of caring about quality. The question isn't how to care less. It's how to tell the difference between reflection (useful) and rumination (exhausting) so you can have one and not the other. Reflection has an end point. Rumination is a circle.
