tier_variant: D

There is a particular form of depression that happens in high-performers. It is not the obvious kind. It is the functional kind. The kind that exists underneath the outputs that continue to be delivered. The person is still working. Still producing. Still appearing to be the same person they were before. The external evidence suggests continuity. The deal closes. The team continues. The results are delivered. But internally, something has gone offline.
