## EXERCISE v01: five_minute_screen_pause
---
Close your laptop right now. Keep phone face-down on desk. Slide your chair back. Stand up from your desk slowly. Press your feet into floor. Squeeze your feet three times. Roll your shoulders backward slowly. Breathe in through your nose. Count to four silently. Breathe out through your mouth. Do this three times. Look away from the screen. Find something across the room. Let your eyes focus on it. Don't look at your phone. Sit back in your chair. Reopen your laptop when ready.

## EXERCISE v02: the_notification_pause
---
Turn off all notifications for sixty seconds. Keep your phone face-down away. Put your hands on your keyboard. Notice the weight of them. Press your palms flat against keyboard. Feel the desk beneath them. Release your hands slowly. Shake them out gently. Roll your wrists in circles. Forward. Backward. Forward again. This releases tension held there. Your anxiety lives in hands. Release it. Take three deep breaths. Watch your chest rise. Watch your chest fall. The notifications will wait. You can breathe first.
