

## EXERCISE v30
The Acceptance of Uncertainty. Step 1: The core of the sleep anxiety is often: discomfort with uncertainty. Step 2: You want to know how things will go. You can't. Step 3: Practice small uncertainties: send something without re-reviewing. Delegate something. Make a quick decision. Step 4: Survive the small uncertainties. Your system learns: uncertainty is survivable. Step 5: The practice during the day changes the nervous system's response at night.
