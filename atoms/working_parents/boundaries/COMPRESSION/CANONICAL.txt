## COMPRESSION v01 | boundary_not_cruelty
---
compression_family: C1
---
A boundary isn't unkind just because someone feels disappointed by it. You can set a limit and still care about the person. The guilt that arrives when you say no isn't evidence that you've done something wrong. It's just the familiar voice that told you your needs were secondary.
