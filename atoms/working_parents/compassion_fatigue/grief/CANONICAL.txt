## STORY v01
---
path: mourning_my_capacity
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
I grieve the parent I intended to be—patient, present, fully engaged. She's gone. I'm the depleted version now. The grief is for her, for the person I used to be before everyone needed me all at once, constantly. I didn't lose her to death but she's lost to me anyway.

## STORY v02
---
path: the_relationships_that_atrophied
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
My friendships are shadows now. The depth I could offer before is gone. I'm mourning the friend I was to people I love. I didn't choose this loss. It happened while I was managing everyone else's emergencies. The grief is quiet but constant.

## STORY v03
---
path: goodbye_to_reciprocity
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF3
---
I'm grieving the relationships that were mutual. Everything is transactional now—I give what I can, which is diminished, and I'm too tired to receive anything back. The reciprocal intimacy I used to have is gone, replaced by something more like obligation. I'm mourning the version of connection that required presence.

BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF4
