## COMPRESSION v01 | depression_is_system_offline
---
compression_family: C1
---
Your system is offline. Not broken. Not permanent. Just currently unavailable. Everything feels flat because your nervous system has shut down the color. Depression is your system saying: too much, too long. Now you rest whether you want to or not.
