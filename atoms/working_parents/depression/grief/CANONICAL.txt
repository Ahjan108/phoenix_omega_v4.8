BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
Everything is the same gray now. Things that should spark joy don't. I have a moment of connection with my partner and I feel the grief that I can't fully meet it with presence. I'm mourning the capacity to feel joy, which seems like it's gone. Or hidden. The grief is quiet and pervasive.

## STORY v02
---
path: loss_of_interest_is_a_kind_of_dying
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
There are hobbies I loved. Books I wanted to read. Conversations I wanted to have. Now they all feel like too much. I'm grieving the version of myself that wanted things. She was still in there a year ago. Now there's mostly just the maintenance version—the one who does what's necessary and doesn't ask for more.

## STORY v03

path: mourning_the_future_i_thought_i'd_have
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF3
---
I thought by now I'd feel more stable. More connected. More like myself. But I'm grieving the version of the future that isn't going to arrive. Instead there's just the same gray day repeated. The grief is for time lost and the acceptance that maybe I'm not getting better. Maybe this is just what it is now.
