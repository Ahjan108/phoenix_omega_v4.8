## STORY v01
---
path: everything_at_once_and_nothing
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
I'm depressed and I still have to be functional. I'm heartbroken and I still have to make dinner. I'm completely done and someone still needs something from me. The overwhelm is that my depression exists inside a life that doesn't pause for it. Nobody can know how bad it is because if they did, everything would fall apart.

## STORY v02
---
path: the_weight_that_won't_lift
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
There's a heaviness that extends to every task. Getting out of bed, responding to messages, showing up. It all feels impossible and yet I'm doing it. The overwhelm is that I'm functioning while drowning. The performance is exhausting. The depression is exhausting. And they're simultaneous.

## STORY v03
---
path: too_much_need_and_i'm_empty
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF3
---
Everyone needs something. My capacity is zero. The overwhelm is the collision between their needs and my emptiness. I can see how much is required and I'm terrified because I don't have it in me. The gap between what's needed and what I can provide feels like it's widening.
