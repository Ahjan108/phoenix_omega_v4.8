## STORY v01
---
path: watching_the_motions
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
I see myself doing the necessary things. I'm functioning. I'm taking care of people. Nobody knows I'm barely here. The watcher watches me perform competence while feeling numb to the whole enterprise. There's a strange safety in the distance. If I watch instead of feel, maybe I won't have to confront how much I'm not okay.

SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2

## STORY v03
---
path: distant_from_my_own_life
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF3
---
It's like watching a movie about someone who has my life. She's my age, she has my family, she does my job. But I'm not in her body; I'm watching from somewhere else. The distance is protective and also lonely. I'm isolated from my own experience by being outside it.
---

## STORY v04
---
path: the_numbness_from_a_distance
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF4
---
From where I'm watching, feeling seems optional. And option I'm not choosing. It's easier to be the observer than to be the person being observed. This detachment is a kind of relief, but also a confirmation that I'm not really living. I'm witnessing life. That's different.
