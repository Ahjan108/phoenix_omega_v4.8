## STORY v01
---
path: the_impostor_in_my_own_life
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
I'm not the person people think I am. I'm the person who forgets things, who yells at her kids, who doesn't have the patience I'm supposed to have. If anyone knew what I'm actually like when nobody's watching, they'd understand that I'm not as competent as I appear. The shame is that I'm not enough and I'm terrified of that becoming visible.

## STORY v02
---
path: smaller_than_i'm_supposed_to_be
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
I should be able to do more. I should be more patient, more present, more successful, more everything. The gap between what I should be and what I am is the landscape of my shame. I'm failing at being the person I'm supposed to be and that failure feels fundamental.

## STORY v03
---
path: the_failure_i_can't_admit
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF3
---
I'm not enjoying this. Motherhood, partnership, work—I'm doing it, but I'm not enjoying it the way I'm supposed to. There's a shame in that admission, in the knowledge that I'm broken in a specific way. I'm supposed to be fulfilled by all this and instead I'm just trying to get through it.
---

## STORY v04
---
path: the_person_i'm_hiding
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF4
---
The real me—tired, angry, overwhelmed, not okay—is the version I can never show. So I hide her. I'm ashamed of her. I'm ashamed that she exists inside me, that she's the actual person under the functioning facade. The shame is that if anyone saw her, they'd know I don't deserve the good things I have.
