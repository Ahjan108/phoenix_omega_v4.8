## STORY v01
---
path: racing_heart_at_lights_out
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
I lie down and feel my heart pulse too hard, too fast. Something's wrong. Am I sick? Is my heart actually pounding or am I hyperaware of a normal heartbeat? Now I'm monitoring it. Now I'm sure something's wrong. I consider waking my partner. I consider going to the ER. The night stretches with me checking my pulse, trying to discern what's real and what's my anxiety about sleep telling me.

BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
I read something about sleep apnea and now I'm listening for it. In myself. All night. Waiting for evidence of something that probably isn't there. My breathing sounds wrong. Or does it? I can't sleep because I'm paying attention to my airway. The awareness I accidentally created is now running the show.
