## STORY v01
---
path: noticing_the_knot
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF1
---
My shoulders are up around my ears. I'm not doing anything stressful right now—I'm just sitting—but my body is braced. It's like I'm constantly expecting impact. I notice this from outside myself: there's my body, always ready to dodge. The noticing doesn't change it, but I'm watching it with something like curiosity now instead of just living inside it.

## STORY v02
---
path: the_jaw_clench
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF2
---
I catch myself: teeth together so hard there's pain. I'm not angry in this moment. But my body is angry. It's carrying anger that might not even be mine—it's the accumulated frustration of a hundred compromised moments. I notice it happening in real-time, which is strange. Usually I only know afterward.

## STORY v03
---
path: breathing_like_i'm_running
BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF3
---
Even in stillness, my breath is shallow and quick. Like I've just escaped something. Or I'm about to have to. My body's nervous system hasn't received the memo that I'm safe. I watch this: the way my breath mirrors an emergency that isn't happening. I'm observing myself live in a story my body hasn't let go of.

## STORY v04

BAND: 1
SEMANTIC_FAMILY: SF4
