**Practice:** She tries loving-kindness breathing before entering the studio. Slow inhale: may I be safe. Exhale: may I be enough. Her ribcage softens on the third round. She sits with it. The doubt does not leave. But the cold in her hands recedes.

**After:** She submits to the gallery on a Tuesday. Not because the doubt disappears. Because she stops waiting for it to leave before she moves. She does not hear back for two weeks. She does not check.
