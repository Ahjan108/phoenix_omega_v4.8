## boundaries_therapist_story

**emotional_intensity_band:** 3
**story_origin:** composite
**story_type:** character_study

**Before:** Carmen spends her days helping others set boundaries. At home she answers every text from her mother within minutes. Even at 11pm. Her stomach knots before she picks up the phone. She tells herself this time will be different. It is not different.

**Practice:** She starts extended exhale breathing before responding. Four counts in, eight counts out. Three rounds. Her hand rests on her chest. Her breathing slows. She does not pick up the phone until the knot loosens.

**After:** She does not stop answering. She starts choosing when. The pause between the buzz and the response grows from zero seconds to ten minutes. Her mother adjusts. Carmen sleeps through the night. The boundary was not words. It was timing.
