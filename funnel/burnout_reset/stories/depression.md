## depression_journalist_story

**emotional_intensity_band:** 4
**story_origin:** composite
**story_type:** character_study

**Before:** Marcus stops pitching stories. Stops answering emails. He tells his editor he works on something long-form. He watches the ceiling fan. His body sinks into the mattress like concrete. His sister calls. He lets it ring.

**Practice:** She sends him breath walking anyway: match your inhale to two steps, exhale to four. He tries it from his bed to the kitchen. Twelve steps. That is the whole practice the first day. He stands at the counter. His feet on the cold tile. He breathes.

**After:** By the third week he walks to the mailbox. By the fifth he walks to the coffee shop and opens his laptop. He does not write that day. But he is there. The ceiling fan is somewhere behind him.
