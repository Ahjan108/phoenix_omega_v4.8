## financial_anxiety_accountant_story

**emotional_intensity_band:** 3
**story_origin:** composite
**story_type:** character_study

**Before:** Grace manages other people's money for a living. Her own accounts sit unopened for four months. The notification badges reach triple digits. Her chest tightens when the banking app icon appears on screen. She moves it to a folder she does not open.

**Practice:** She starts triangle breathing before opening her laptop. Four counts in, hold two, six counts out. Her hand rests on the trackpad. Not yet clicking. She breathes three more rounds. Her chest softens enough to click.

**After:** She opens the app on a Wednesday. The numbers land hard but survivable. She makes a plan the same afternoon. The avoidance cost her more than the debt. The looking was cheaper than the hiding.
