## imposter_syndrome_director_story

**emotional_intensity_band:** 3
**story_origin:** composite
**story_type:** character_study

**Before:** Amara receives two promotions in eighteen months. Each time, the first thought: now they see. She prepares for every meeting like a defense. Her back tightens before she enters the conference room. She memorizes numbers she already knows. The preparation is not about being ready. It is about not being caught.

**Practice:** Before her next all-hands she tries the sigh of relief in the stairwell. One deep inhale, then a long audible exhale. Mouth open. Shoulders dropping. Three times. She stands still. The tightness in her back loosens one notch.

**After:** She still prepares. But she stops rehearsing the version of herself she thinks they need. Her next quarterly review uses the word 'authentic' for the first time. She does not know what changed. They do.
