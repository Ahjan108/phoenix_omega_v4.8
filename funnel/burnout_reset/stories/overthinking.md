## overthinking_lawyer_story

**emotional_intensity_band:** 3
**story_origin:** composite
**story_type:** character_study

**Before:** Daniel replays closing arguments every night. Not preparing. Looping. The same words, the same what-ifs. His jaw locks at 2am. His teeth ache by morning. He snaps at his associate over a filing error that does not matter. She stops bringing him drafts.

**Practice:** He tries 4-7-8 breathing at his desk after court. Four counts in, hold for seven, exhale for eight. The extended exhale pulls against his chest. His jaw unlocks. He sits with it. His body resists letting go. That resistance is the signal.

**After:** By the second week the loops shorten. They stop following him to bed. His associate starts bringing drafts again. He does not mention why anything changed. His jaw unclenches.
