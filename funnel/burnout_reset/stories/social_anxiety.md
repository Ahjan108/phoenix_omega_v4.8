## social_anxiety_developer_story

**emotional_intensity_band:** 3
**story_origin:** composite
**story_type:** character_study

**Before:** Raj declines standup invites for three months. His camera stays off. His manager assumes disengagement. She mentions it in his one-on-one. His throat closes. He says he will work on it. His throat stays closed.

**Practice:** He tries bee breath in his car before the office. Lips closed. Long exhale with a hum. The vibration in his chest loosens the band around his throat after four rounds. He sits with it. The hum lingers in his sternum.

**After:** He turns his camera on the following Monday. Not for the whole meeting. Just the first five minutes. Nobody mentions it. By the end of the month he stops rehearsing what to say before unmuting. The preparation was the prison.
